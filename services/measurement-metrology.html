Our Services

Measurement & Metrology

Non-contact dimensional verification at micron-level accuracy — fast, reliable, and fully traceable.

Overview

Measure Without Touching

Machine vision metrology delivers fast, non-contact dimensional measurement of components — replacing or complementing traditional CMMs and manual gauges. Systems measure at speed, inline, with results available immediately after each part passes.

Unlike contact measurement, vision-based systems introduce no risk of marking or distorting delicate surfaces. They operate at production line speed, inspecting 100% of parts rather than relying on statistical sampling.

Opsistech builds metrology systems calibrated to traceable standards, with reporting tailored to your quality management and compliance requirements.

Capabilities

What We Measure

Linear Dimensions

Length, width, height, and diameter measured to micron-level precision — verified against nominal values and tolerance bands.

Geometric Tolerances

Flatness, straightness, roundness, cylindricity, and parallelism assessed to GD&T standards — ensuring parts conform to engineering intent.

Gap & Flush

Automotive panel gap and flush measurement to sub-millimetre accuracy — critical for body-in-white and door assembly quality.

Thread & Profile

Non-contact measurement of thread pitch, form, and depth — as well as gear tooth profiles and complex cross-sections.

3D Surface Mapping

Full surface point clouds compared against CAD reference data — identifying deviations across the entire part geometry, not just discrete points.

SPC & Reporting

Statistical process control charts, Cpk analysis, and trend monitoring — turning measurement data into actionable process intelligence.

Process

How It Works

01
Part Presentation Parts are presented to the measurement system via fixture, conveyor, or robotic handling. For inline systems, measurement occurs without stopping the production flow.
02
Calibrated Imaging Cameras and optics are geometrically calibrated to remove lens distortion and establish a precise, traceable mapping between image pixels and real-world dimensions.
03
Sub-pixel Feature Detection Edges, circles, corners, and profiles are located to sub-pixel accuracy using advanced gradient and fitting algorithms — achieving measurement repeatability far beyond the pixel resolution of the camera.
04
Dimension Extraction & Comparison Geometric calculations extract all required dimensions from the detected features. Each value is compared against nominal dimensions and tolerance limits — yielding a pass, fail, or out-of-trend result.
05
Data Logging & Reporting All measurement results are archived with part ID, timestamp, and operator data. Reports, SPC charts, and capability studies are generated automatically for quality review and audit.

Critical dimensional verification of implants, instruments, and housings — with full traceability to ISO 13485 and regulatory requirements.

Electronics & Connectors

Pin height, coplanarity, pitch, and contact geometry verified on connectors, PCBs, and semiconductor packages.
