Consistent, high-speed defect detection — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with zero fatigue.

Overview

What is Automated Visual Inspection?

Automated visual inspection uses industrial cameras, precision lighting, and advanced image processing algorithms to examine products at every stage of production — faster and more consistently than any human operator.

Where manual inspection is subject to fatigue, distraction, and variability, machine vision delivers the same precise judgement on the first unit as on the ten-thousandth. Every product is checked. Every defect is flagged. Every decision is logged.

Opsistech designs and deploys automated inspection systems tailored to your production environment — from high-speed packaging lines to precision electronics assembly.

Capabilities

What We Detect

Surface Defects

Scratches, dents, cracks, discolouration, and contamination — detected at micron-level sensitivity across any material.

Shape Anomalies

Deformed, bent, or incorrectly shaped components identified against a known-good reference model.

Missing Components

Absent parts, fasteners, labels, or printed elements flagged immediately — before the product moves to the next stage.

Faulty Electronics

PCB solder defects, misaligned components, lifted pins, and incorrect polarity detected at board and component level.

Label & Print Verification

Correct placement, legibility, and content of labels, date codes, lot numbers, and barcodes confirmed automatically.

Packaging Integrity

Seal quality, fill level, cap presence, and tamper evidence verified across bottles, blisters, pouches, and cartons.

Process

How It Works

01
Image Acquisition Industrial cameras — area scan, line scan, or 3D — capture high-resolution images of each product as it moves along the production line. Triggering is synchronised with line speed, ensuring no unit is missed.
02
Controlled Illumination Precision lighting — structured, diffuse, backlit, or coaxial — is selected to make defects visible while suppressing surface variation. The right light makes the invisible obvious.
03
Image Processing & Analysis Vision algorithms — edge detection, pattern matching, blob analysis, and AI-based classification — analyse each image in milliseconds, comparing against defined acceptance criteria.
04
Pass / Fail Decision & Rejection Non-conforming parts trigger an automated rejection mechanism — air blast, push rod, or diverter — removing them from the line without interrupting production. All results are logged for traceability.
