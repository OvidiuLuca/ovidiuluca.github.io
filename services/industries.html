Industries Served

Machine vision expertise deployed across Romania's key industrial sectors.

Overview

Sector-Specific Experience

Machine vision is not one-size-fits-all. Each industry operates under different constraints — different line speeds, different regulatory requirements, different defect types, different tolerances for false rejects and escapes.

Opsistech brings sector-specific knowledge to every project — understanding not just the technology, but the standards, the workflows, and the commercial pressures of each environment.

Industry

Automotive

The automotive sector demands zero-defect manufacturing at high volumes. Safety-critical components must be inspected 100% — there is no sampling, no margin for error.

Opsistech systems deliver full traceability, SPC integration, and audit-ready data for every part inspected.

  • Body panel surface inspection
  • Weld quality verification
  • Fastener and clip presence checks
  • Brake and safety component examination
  • Robot guidance for assembly cells
  • Part identification and serialisation

Industry

Electronics

Electronics manufacturing requires inspection at scales invisible to the human eye — solder joints measured in microns, component placements accurate to fractions of a millimetre.

From solder paste inspection (SPI) before reflow to automated optical inspection (AOI) after, Opsistech covers the full PCB assembly process.

  • Solder paste inspection (SPI)
  • Automated optical inspection (AOI)
  • Component placement verification
  • Connector pin and polarity checks
  • Label and marking verification
  • 3D inspection of solder joints

Industry

Food & Beverage

Food and beverage production runs at high speed with zero tolerance for contamination, underfills, or mislabelling. Vision systems must operate in challenging environments — wet, cold, and fast.

All Opsistech systems deployed in food environments are designed to food-grade hygiene standards, with IP-rated enclosures and wash-down compatible components.

  • Fill level verification
  • Cap and seal integrity
  • Foreign body detection
  • Label placement and legibility
  • Date and lot code verification
  • Shape and weight estimation

Industry

Logistics

Modern logistics operations move thousands of items per hour. Vision systems read codes, verify destinations, measure dimensions, and guide automated handling — all at conveyor speed.

From parcel sortation centres to warehouse automation, Opsistech delivers the reading accuracy and system reliability that logistics operations demand.

  • Barcode and QR reading at speed
  • Parcel dimensioning and volume
  • Sortation verification
  • Damage detection on incoming goods
  • Robot guidance for picking
  • Address and label verification

Industry

Pharmaceuticals

Pharmaceutical manufacturing is among the most regulated in the world. Every unit must be inspected, every result logged, and every system validated to GMP and 21 CFR Part 11 requirements.

Opsistech delivers compliant vision systems with full audit trails, electronic records, and the validation documentation required for regulated pharmaceutical environments.

  • Blister pack completeness
  • Tablet colour, shape, and size
  • Serialisation and 2D code reading
  • Label content and placement
  • Vial and ampoule inspection
  • Seal and cap integrity
